The Problem

Transportation operators are experiencing about 40% profit loss because of wasteful resource utilization and allocation.

They are using inadequate human processes and systems that are only partially effective in making quick and accurate operational decisions in a digital world.

Reduce Resource Waste by about 40%

Predictive Logistic Adaptive Intelligence (PLAI) is an AI-based software that dynamically optimizes asset utilization and allocation. It is able to operate one or multiple transporation networks in tandem and in real-time, resulting in increased efficiency and company profits.

This technology is applied in transportation industry applications such as for Addison Lee Taxi, Rus Global Trucking and others in the logistics industry. The software can be adapted and become particularly beneficial to the new and upcoming jet taxi sharing model where many variables directly affect profitability.

How does "PLAI" prevent air taxi sharing companies from losing 40% profit margin?

Within the air taxi sharing environment, many resource utilization and allocation decisions require quick analysis of multiple data sets across various platforms, simultaneously. Time, money and resources are at stake.

Our AI-based technology can gather, analyze, provide and execute the best options faster and better than its human counterpart, therefore optimizing use of time, money and resources.
